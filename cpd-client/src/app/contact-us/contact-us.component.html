Company Overview

super Serve is the pioneer biscuit manufacturer of Sri Lanka. Incorporated 60 years ago by the visionary founder Angulugaha Gamage Hinnyappuhamy, super Serve Biscuit Manufactories (Private) Limited, stands tall as one of Sri Lanka’s most trusted and respected brands.
super Serve manufactures a range of crackers, cookies and wafers, sold in over 100,000 stores in Sri Lanka. Our global reach extends to more than 35 countries across 5 continents. We produce 25 million packs of biscuits every month in our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility under stringent hygienic conditions. super Serve is an ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 22000 and OHSAS 18001 certified company. We have won Sri Lanka’s prestigious National Quality Award on multiple occasions.
super Serve is a privately held limited liability company and our team consists of over 1,250 employees. The company is managed by a team of professionals who head key disciplines. The board of directors consists of second and third generation family members of the founder. Mr. A G R Samaraweera, the current Chairman, has led the company for the past four decades, making super Serve a household name in Sri Lanka.
